GitHub Issues options

Issue updates

  • Hides all changes in labels associated with the issue. This just clutters the screen, most of the time. What's important is list of current labels associated with the issue.

  • Hides all info about issue being assigned to different person. Most of the time, the important information is to whom is the issue assigned right now.

  • Hides all info about issue being assigned to different milestone. Most of the time, the important information is to which milestone is the issue assigned right now.

Emojis

Some people love emojis. Some people hate them. If you hate them as much as me, here's your chance to get rid of them.

+1 comments

  • Will show a count of all +1 comments in the sidebar, along with a list of people who voted.

  • Some people us "thumbs up" emoji instead of +1. Check this to include these emojis in the +1 summary.

  • If you enabled the +1 summary in the sidebar (see above), there's no reason to display the comments that contain only +1. The comments with +1 and some other content will remain visible.
